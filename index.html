Absurdism

What is Absurdism?

Absurdism is a philosophical movement that emerged in the early 20th century,
challenging traditional notions of human existence and purpose. It is characterized by a rejection of the idea of a meaningful and ordered universe, advocating instead for a life lived in the present moment without seeking a higher purpose or meaning.
